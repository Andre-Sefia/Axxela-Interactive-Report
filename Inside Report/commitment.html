Our Sustainability Commitment

Our primary mission is the accelerated access to clean, efficient and affordable energy across the continent, and globally. We design, finance, build and operate natural gas and power infrastructure that is critical to improving people’s wellbeing and driving sustainable economic development.

Trailblazing sustainable strategies

Axxela is positioned as a forerunner in the fields of natural gas, power, and energy infrastructure development, working assiduously to illuminate the path toward a sustainable future.

Reflecting on our sustainability journey

We achieved sustainability by carefully planning, overcoming challenges, and committing to growth while fully integrating ESG principles into our operations.

Preserving biodiversity and natural habitats

Explore More

Our operations protect the environment and reduce potential ecosystem impacts. We accomplish this through a multi-faceted approach that conducts both environmental impact and social assessments in the planning and pre-construction stages of new opportunities.

Water conservation and responsible usage

Explore More

Water resource usage in our operations supports only necessary administrative functions and produces no significant environmental effects and obtain drinking water responsibly from underground sources through boreholes and from vendors.
