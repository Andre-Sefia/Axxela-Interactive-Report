Community Empowerment

COMMUNITY EMPOWERMENT

Axxela's Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives are aimed at promoting positive economic, social, and environmental changes in communities where we operate. These initiatives focus on three priority areas as outlined in the CSR policy: healthcare, education, and the provision of potable water. Additional areas are considered based on the specific needs of the local community.

Supplier engagement and responsible sourcing

Explore More

Axxela ensures vendors align with EHSSS policies and global standards, reducing emissions through cleaner fuels and supporting Nigeria's local content laws to boost local suppliers and jobs. Supply chain compliance is reinforced through policies, newsletters, forums, and site visits, with effectiveness monitored via inspections, assessments, and vendor appraisals.
