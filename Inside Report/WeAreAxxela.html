WE ARE AXXELA

Who We Are

At Axxela, we take pride in being a trailblazer in the energy and utilities sector in sub-Saharan Africa. We have a strong commitment to innovation, operational excellence, and sustainable development, and we are charting the course for the region's energy transition. A jointly co-owned entity of Helios Investment Partners LLP and Sojitz Corporation, Axxela is anchored at the intersection of local know-how and international time-tested approaches, allowing us to deliver forward-thinking solutions that respond to the world's evolving energy requirements.

Our primary mission is the accelerated access to clean, efficient and affordable energy across the continent, and globally. We design, finance, build and operate natural gas and power infrastructure that is critical to improving people's wellbeing and driving sustainable economic development.
