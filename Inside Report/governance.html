GOVERNANCE AND ETHICAL LEADERSHIP

At Axxela, governance is not just structure, it is a commitment to ethical leadership that shapes sustainable growth. Our Board of Directors serves as the highest governance authority, guiding the company's environmental, social, and economic direction. In 2024, the Board was comprised of one Executive Director (the CEO) and seven Non-Executive Directors, including one female director. They bring diverse expertise across energy, infrastructure, and global policy, supported by three key board committees.
Our CEO provides quarterly ESG updates to the Board, ensuring continuous alignment between operations and our sustainability priorities. All sustainability disclosures are reviewed and approved by the Board before publication. The Chairman of Axxela Group, Omoboyede Olusanya, is a seasoned business leader with extensive experience in strategic leadership across finance, infrastructure, and telecommunications. He brings a strong track record in corporate transformation and capital markets, guiding Axxela's vision of delivering sustainable energy solutions across West Africa.

In driving compliance, Axxela upholds global best practices through a suite of governance policies, including Anti-Corruption, Business Conduct and Ethics, KYC, CSR, and more. These frameworks shape our ethical culture, reinforce stakeholder trust, and ensure our operations contribute meaningfully to sustainable development.
