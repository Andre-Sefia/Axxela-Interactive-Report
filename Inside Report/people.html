Prioritising employee health and safety

Explore More

Axxela emphasizes environmental protection and health and safety across its operations by complying with NIS ISO 14001:2015 and ISO 45001:2018 standards. The company encourages staff involvement in improving its Environmental, Occupational Health, and Safety Management System. This approach focuses on hazard identification, risk control, and compliance with laws and best practices to ensure a safe, clean, and environmentally responsible workplace.
