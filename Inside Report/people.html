PEOPLE AND WORKPLACE CULTURE

Employee engagement and wellbeing

Explore More

The company prioritizes employee well-being through programs that support physical health, emotional stability, and financial security. These initiatives empower employees to manage their well-being while preparing for a secure and fulfilling future. During the reporting period, the workforce included 123 individuals—101 permanent employees and 22 temporary staff. Employee demographics are detailed in accompanying illustrations.

Compensation, benefit and employee support

Explore More

Axxela fosters a high-performing and motivated workforce through a structured pay system that includes both fixed and variable components. Fixed pay is set between the 50th and 75th market percentiles, while variable pay is tied to annual performance goals and includes bonuses and awards. The company also offers a wide range of inclusive benefits for all employees. A key achievement during the reporting year was the revision of the Parental Leave Policy, granting 10 days of leave to fathers—highlighting Axxela's commitment to work-life balance and family support.

Prioritising employee health and safety

Explore More

Axxela emphasizes environmental protection and health and safety across its operations by complying with NIS ISO 14001:2015 and ISO 45001:2018 standards. The company encourages staff involvement in improving its Environmental, Occupational Health, and Safety Management System. This approach focuses on hazard identification, risk control, and compliance with laws and best practices to ensure a safe, clean, and environmentally responsible workplace.

Advancing diversity, equity, and inclusion (DEI)

Explore More

Axxela values diversity and inclusion for their positive impact on decision-making, work practices, and bias reduction, while promoting gender and cultural representation across the organization. Acknowledging potential risks like stereotyping and discrimination, the company's Diversity Policy includes regular monitoring, enforcement measures, and performance evaluations. Initiatives such as tailored job applications, targeted training, and development programs help mitigate negative effects and support progress. Notably, no discrimination incidents were reported during the year, and the Board remains committed to improving diversity outcomes.
